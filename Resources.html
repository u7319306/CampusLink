🅿️ Parking at ANU

The ANU Acton campus offers a range of parking options for staff, students, and visitors. Most parking areas are managed by CellOPark, which allows cashless payment through the mobile app.

  • Pay-as-you-go parking – Available in general and visitor zones across campus.
  • Permit parking – Staff and students can apply for long-term parking permits.
  • Accessible parking – Dedicated spaces for mobility permit holders near main buildings.
  • Motorcycle parking – Free spaces in several car parks.
  • Electric vehicle charging – Available near North Road and Kambri areas.

More info: ANU Parking Options

📚 Study Spaces

Looking for the perfect spot to study, collaborate, or focus? Here are some of the best spaces on campus — from quiet libraries to vibrant study hubs.

Chifley Library

Quiet and focused environment with individual desks and power outlets. Great for solo study and research.

Hours: 8am – 11pm

Location: Chifley Place

Hancock Library

Spacious and bright, ideal for science and engineering students. Includes bookable group rooms and quiet areas.

Hours: 8am – 10pm

Location: Science Precinct

Kambri Study Spaces

Casual, open spaces with natural light and nearby cafes. Perfect for group discussions or a quick review between classes.

Hours: 24/7

Location: Kambri Precinct
